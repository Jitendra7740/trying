Solar Energy

What is Solar Energy?

Solar energy is energy from the sun that is converted into thermal or electrical energy. Solar energy is a renewable energy source, and it is one of the most widely used forms of energy today.

How is Solar Energy Used?

Solar energy can be used for a variety of purposes, including heating, cooling, electricity production, and even desalination. Solar energy can be used to power homes, businesses, and even entire cities.

Benefits of Solar Energy

Solar energy is a clean, renewable energy source that can help reduce greenhouse gas emissions. It is also cost-effective, as it does not require expensive fuels or infrastructure. In addition, solar energy is abundant and available almost everywhere on Earth.
